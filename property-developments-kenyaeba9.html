Vantage Square

Thika Road
Vantage Square
From Kshs 3.5m
 
Facebook Twitter Google +
Expand All

Vantage Square is a ground breaking new development that will bring heightened vibrancy and energy to Thika Superhighways residential, social and retail landscape. Ideally situated at the convergence of Garden Estate and Thika Superhighway, this meticulously planned development of inspired apartments is beautifully appointed and thoughtfully laid out, capturing leafy district and city skyline views while lively pedestrian realms nurture a new community hub below. With exquisite architectural work, the brilliantly curvaceous exterior hints at the grandeur within. In all ways, Vantage square welcomes a new era for urban luxury living.

Anticipated completion: Quarter 4, 2020

 Living

Enjoy the elegance of open living spaces, invigorating yet timeless with graceful and a seamless feel. Every apartment a Vantage Square will feature premium finishes with layouts and orientation designed to capture the best outlooks. Everything is in place for you to experience the lifestyle you want, your way.

Shopping

Drop by the shops at moments notice, grab a coffee with friends or sit down to an alfresco brunch and enjoy the charming cafe culture that enlivens the retail plaza. Vantage square will feature two levels of retail space including a supermarket, restaurants and fashion stores bringing together all the elements of an engaging city lifestyle

Leisure

The raised landscaped leisure zone offers stunning views over the city and suburbs with expansive decks creating a resort like mood with an array of astounding amenities:

  • Visitors Reception and Lounge
  • Executive Residents Lounge
  • Dramatic Wraparound Swimming Pool
  • Fully Equipped Gym
  • Indoor Games Room
  • Landscaped Gardens
  • Gazebos and Fire Pit
  • Children’s Play Area
  • Outdoor Gardens Seating

Extras

  • Borehole
  • Water Storage Facilities
  • Solar Hot Water Systems
  • Satellite TV and Fibre Optic Infrastructure
  • Power Backup for Common Areas
  • Intercom
  • Electric Fencing
  • Secure Parking for Residents and Visitors
  • CCTV Surveillance
  • Access Control Facility at Lobby
  • Manned Security at Entrance
  • High Speed Lift
  • Pressurized Fire Escape
  • Disabled Access
  • On-site Management Office

Vantage Square provides the investor with a combination of premium location and high grade product within a mixed use development supplying a niche unserved in the area, all of which make the trappings of an excellent investment. Capital gains and rental yields are forecasted to be high considering the ease of access from the major roads that this property provides coupled with the sustained demand from young professionals looking for city fringe living.
