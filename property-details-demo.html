Full description

A spacious 1 bedroom unfurnished apartment Located on Upper Garth road in Bangor with great views across the bay and towards the mountains. The property is made up of a large double bedroom, lounge, kitchen and bathroom. The apartment is served via gas central heating and is fully double glazed.
